State-of-the-Art Facilities

At Modern Times, we provide our team with state-of-the-art equipment and technology to fuel creativity and innovation. Our facilities support both Android and iOS systems, ensuring that our games reach a wide audience of players.

Location

Located at 1221 Alamitos Avenue in Long Branch, NJ, our headquarters is housed in a newly LEED-certified building. Join us in a vibrant and dynamic work environment that fosters collaboration and growth.

Competitive Salary and Benefits

We offer a competitive salary that is in line with industry standards, along with exceptional medical coverage to support your well-being. Additionally, employees enjoy four weeks of vacation time per year*, allowing for ample time to recharge and explore personal interests. Benefits per year include 2 weeks paid community service time each year.

Community Engagement

At Modern Times, we believe in giving back to our community. That's why we provide two weeks of paid community service time each year, empowering our team to make a positive impact beyond the office walls.
